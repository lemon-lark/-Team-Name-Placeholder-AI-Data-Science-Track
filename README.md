# The Problem:
# Apartment hunting is often an overwhelming and tedious process, with prospective residents struggling to find their perfect home. Most rental market platforms have extremely limiting filters, only focusing on a few basic parameters, such as location, size, price, and building amenities. However, buyers typically consider many other criteria, ranging from neighborhood demographics to proximity to worship sites. This forces them to conduct manual research across various platforms for each apartment in order to determine if it fits their needs. 
# The Solution:
# AIPartments tailors search results to the exact needs of the user, prioritizing what they care about the most. Renters who prefer to live near places of worship or in a community that reflects their identity will be able to easily find those apartments. Having everything in one site ensures accessibility, and including diverse datasets streamlines the apartment search.
